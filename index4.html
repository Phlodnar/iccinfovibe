Talangpaz Building

The Talangpaz building (name after the A.R. Foundresses, Mother Dionisia and Mother Cecilia Rosa Talangapaz) was constructed and was blessed on December 2, 2007.

Talangpaz Ground Floor

Talangpaz's ground level has the principal office and treasurer's office, where you pay your tuition costs, as well as the guidance office and registrar, which handle student entrance, registration, and grade reporting. And they also have an emergency room in case injuries or sickness occur.

Talangpaz Front Lobby

In the Talangpaz Front Lobby, an image of the blessed Virgin Mary greets you as you enter the building, as well as pictures of teachers that are teaching in it.

Talangpaz Registrar Office

The Registrar's Office at Talangpaz oversees crucial aspects of student administration, including the management of student records, enrollment processes, class schedules, and the accurate reporting of grades.
